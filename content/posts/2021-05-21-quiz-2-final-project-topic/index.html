#Final Project Topic: Discipline and Rules in Schools

The Junior High and High School years are arguably the most important for a young person as they determine the course of their future. One of the most important parts of this experience is learning discipline in understanding expectations and adhering to set rules and guidelines within the school they are part of. Using past data from rules infractions, I would like to investigate any patterns that we might see among students. For example, are there certain guidelines they are struggling to follow and understand more than others? If so, should the school or institution consider making any changes or adjustments to these particular rules? Also of interest would be is there a particular grade level that produces more infractions and what type of infractions are they? Does gender play a role in behavior or is it random from year to year? These are important questions for the development of students in order to provide the most productive and growth stimulating environments.
